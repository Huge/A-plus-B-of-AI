Task: For each word in the input, output that word in a simple ASCII box, one box per line.

Example 1
Input:
hello world
Output:
+-----+
|hello|
+-----+
+-----+
|world|
+-----+

Example 2
Input:
Ahoj svete
Output:
+----+
|Ahoj|
+----+
+-----+
|svete|
+-----+

Example 3
Input:
GPT-4o mini
Output:
+------+
|GPT-4o|
+------+
+----+
|mini|
+----+
